My Process

  • UX Research

    It starts with the endeavor to marry business needs with user needs. This requires research methodologies, such as user testing, benchmarking, design sprints, or some creative prodding to discover a clear path to success.

  • UI Design

    I'm passionate about good design and strive for aesthetic products with a top-notch user experience. This phase requires a great understanding of Design Principles, empathy, reiteration, and tools, such as Adobe XD.

  • Development

    When the design successfully addresses the business requirements and user needs, I bring it to life with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The result is a functional, performant, accessible semantic, and SEO-friendly product ready to production.

  • Share

    Every project is an opportunity to learn something new. I love to learn, but I also love to teach. Rather than keep everything to myself, I share what I learn with others on my blog. Helping others succeed is one of the most rewarding parts of the entire process.
